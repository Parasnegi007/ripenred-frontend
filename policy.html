Privacy Commitment

This Privacy Policy explains how Ripe'n Red collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

  • • Name, email, phone number, shipping and billing addresses

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device

  • • IP address, device type, OS, browser

Usage

How We Use Your Information

Service & Fulfillment

  • • Account creation and management

Improvement & Security

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can control cookies in your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We share information with trusted service providers strictly for order fulfillment, payments, analytics, and customer support. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Security

Security Measures

  • • HTTPS/TLS for data in transit
  • • Principle of least privilege
  • • Regular backups and monitoring

Payments

  • • Payments handled via PCI-DSS compliant gateways
  • • We never store full card details

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion where applicable
  • Restrict: Limit certain processing
  • Portability: Receive data in a machine-readable format

  • Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child, we will delete it.

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a revised effective date. Continued use of the service after changes indicates acceptance.
