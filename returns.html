Return & Refund Policy

Ripe'n Red

Last updated: June 12, 2025

Fresh Produce Policy

Important Policy Notice

At Ripe'n Red, we grow and deliver fresh farm produce directly to your doorstep. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we follow a strict return and cancellation policy as outlined below.

No Returns

We do not accept returns on any product once delivered, as our items are perishable and meant for immediate consumption. Please ensure someone is available to receive the order at the scheduled delivery time.

Perishable Products

Due to the fresh nature of our produce, all sales are final upon delivery.

No Cancellations

Orders once placed cannot be canceled or modified, as we begin harvesting and packing immediately to ensure optimal freshness and timely delivery.

Immediate Processing

We start preparing your order immediately after confirmation to ensure maximum freshness.

Refunds (Conditional)

Refunds are issued only in genuine cases where our service standards have not been met:

Wrong Product

Incorrect items delivered due to our error

Damaged Items

Products damaged during transit

Non-Delivery

Complete failure to deliver your order

Refund Process

  • Contact our Help Center within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide photographic evidence and order details
  • Our support team will review and verify the case
  • Full or partial refund approved based on case severity

Refund Processing Timeline

Processing Time

  • Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds are issued to your original payment method

Delayed Refunds

  • If refund is delayed, please check with your bank first
  • If issues persist, contact us at riipenred@gmail.com

Quality Commitment

We stand behind the quality of our produce. Each order is hand-picked and packed fresh. If you're dissatisfied with any aspect of your order, we encourage you to reach out—we're here to make it right.

Our Promise

Every product is carefully selected and packed with love from our farm to ensure you receive only the finest quality produce.

Contact Us

For any refund-related queries or concerns, feel free to reach out to our support team. We're here to help!

Time-Sensitive Issues

For delivery issues or damaged products, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for fastest resolution.
