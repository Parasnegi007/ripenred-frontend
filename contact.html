Frequently Asked Questions

What makes these fruits special?

Our product is 100% natural, handpicked, and sun-ripened to ensure the highest quality.

Where is this product sourced from?

Our fruits are sourced directly from organic farms, ensuring freshness and quality.

How should I store this product?

Keep it in a cool, dry place. Refrigeration helps extend freshness.

What is the shelf life of this product?

The shelf life depends on storage conditions. Typically, our fruits stay fresh for up to 7 days at room temperature and longer when refrigerated.

How is the product delivered?

We offer fast and secure delivery, ensuring the product reaches you in optimal condition. Shipping options vary based on location.

Is this product organic?

Some of our fruits are organic, while others are conventionally grown. The product details will specify whether it is organic.

Should I wash the product before consuming?

Yes, it is always recommended to wash fruits thoroughly before eating to remove any natural residue or external contaminants.

Are all fruits long-lasting?

No, some fruits like cherries and strawberries are highly perishable. It's best to consume them within a few days or refrigerate for extended freshness.

Where is the company based?

Our company operates from Khadrala, Himachal Pradesh, situated at an altitude of 10,000 ft above sea level. The pristine mountain environment ensures top-quality produce.

Why is Khadrala ideal for growing fruits?

Khadrala's high altitude, cool climate, and clean air create the perfect conditions for growing nutrient-rich and flavorful fruits, especially apples, cherries, and berries.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we deliver across India. International shipping is under consideration as we explore logistics and preservation techniques.

How do you ensure quality and freshness?

Each fruit is carefully handpicked, packed fresh, and transported under temperature-controlled conditions to maintain optimal quality.

What makes your fruits unique compared to others?

Our fruits come from high-altitude farms, grown with minimal pesticides, and ripened naturally, ensuring superior taste, texture, and nutritional value.

Do you have a return policy?

We take customer satisfaction seriously! If there's an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll assist with a replacement or refund.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach out via email or phone, and our team will be happy to assist you with any queries or concerns.

What is your delivery time?

Our delivery time ranges from 1-2 days depending on your location.
