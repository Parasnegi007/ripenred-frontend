Bridging the Gap Between Farmers and Consumers

At our core, we aim to connect farmers and consumers directly. Himalayan farms produce fruits of unmatched quality, but middlemen often limit farmers' profits. By delivering fresh harvests straight from the source, we ensure fairness for farmers and unmatched freshness for you. Together, we’re building trust and celebrating nature’s purity.

Empowering Farmers, Enriching lives

By empowering our producers with better returns and providing consumers with high-quality produce at lower costs, we aim to create a sustainable cycle of prosperity.For the producers, this means gaining access to new markets without the burden of exploitative practices. They are empowered to invest in better farming techniques, secure their livelihoods, and dream of a more prosperous future.

Growing Together a Better Tomorrow

Our mission doesn’t stop here. We envision a future where farms across the region unite, joining forces to scale this initiative and share the richness of nature far and wide.This initiative is not just about farming—it’s about creating a legacy. By scaling this model, we hope to revolutionize the way agriculture supports rural communities, ensuring they are no longer overlooked but instead celebrated for their contributions. Through innovation, unity, and a shared commitment to excellence, we aspire to expand beyond the Himalayas, taking the flavors of our fruits and the ethos of fairness to the world.

Where Tradition Meets Tomorrow

Nestled in the majestic heights of the Himalayas, Royal Beauty Orchard Tutupani (RBO Tutupani) been thriving since the 1970s. What began as a small orchard cultivating apples and cherries grew into a cherished family legacy. Founded by the visionary Mr. Kehar Singh Negi, the orchard became a beacon of dedication and perseverance, with harvests reaching fruit markets across India.

Over the years, the orchard has grown into a vibrant sanctuary of fruits Today, we merge modern horticulture techniques with traditional practices to preserve nature's essence while embracing innovation. Today, RBO Tutupani symbolizes the perfect blend of tradition and innovation. Expanding beyond its original offerings, the orchard now produces a rich variety of fruits, including plums, persimmons, blueberries, and strawberries. By embracing modern horticulture techniques and sustainable practices, we aim to preserve nature’s treasures while building a brighter future for farming.
